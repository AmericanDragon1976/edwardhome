    Yin Yang

    Give and take, Ebb and flow,
    Up and down, High and low.

    Beat the odds, Rise to the top.
    Crash and burn, No way to stop.

    Fight your war, Every single day.
    Learn from falls, Find a way.

    Before you win, Comes the pain.
    Can't give up, Too much to gain.

    Along the way, We all have cried.
    Some got up, Some just died.

    To know joy, We must know sorrow,
    Yet look ahead, Hope in Tomorrow.

    I have soared, But didn't win.
    Every dive, Means Climb again.


    By Edward Moroni Goodson
    last revision 10 Jan 2011


        This is one of my favorite poems. Dealing simply with the reality of life. I think the concepts here are such that everyone can relate to some part of the poem, if not all of it. Enjoy.
