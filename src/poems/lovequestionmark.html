    Love?

    One day I stopped and chanced to think,
    Unrequited Love is life's greatest pain.
    Why do we let love exist at all?
    With all that hurt what do we gain?

    Then I thought once again that day,
    LOVE is the greatest joy in life,
    Without it the world would be destroyed.
    All would be full of chaos, hate and strife.

    Love completes the souls of all mankind.
    It binds the wounds of many hearts.
    Healing, helping build all thats good.
    LOVE is where every good thing starts!


    By Edward Moroni Goodson
